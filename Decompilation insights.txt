Forager agitation ("checkAgitation")

	Tapping a forager agitates them
	If they're too agitated
		they squirt muck when tapped
			this resets an 1.1 second cooldown
		they won't jump until they cool down
	clicked foragers "hyperventilate"
		elastic scale in and out

Muck spread ("nextspread")
	slows when muck count > 5
	game states ("gamestate")
		start, birth, growth, maturity
		affects muck spread ("spreadmuck")
